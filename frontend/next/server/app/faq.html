About IntellMatch

IntellMatch is an intelligent relationship management platform powered by AI that helps you identify who matters in your network, where your interests align, and how to turn meetings into real opportunities.

LinkedIn helps you meet new people. CRM systems are used to store contacts. IntellMatch helps you make decisions by analyzing context, interests, and compatibility points to highlight your highest-value relationships.

You define your interests and goals. When you meet someone new, IntellMatch analyzes their publicly available information and matches it with your profile, showing you alignment points, compatibility score, and the best next step.
