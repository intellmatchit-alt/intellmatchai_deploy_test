1. Introduction

IntellMatch ("Platform", "IntellMatch", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its users ("Users", "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, store, share, and protect personal data when you access, register for, or use the Platform. By using IntellMatch, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

2. Controller Identity and Jurisdiction

IntellMatch is registered and operating in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and acts as the Data Controller for personal data processed under this Privacy Policy, unless explicitly stated otherwise. This Privacy Policy is governed primarily by the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and its implementing regulations issued by SDAIA. Where IntellMatch offers services to, or monitors the behavior of, individuals located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, relevant provisions of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable local data protection laws may also apply.

3. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all IntellMatch services, including: • Website and web applications • Mobile applications • Dashboards, tools, and features • Communications and support interactions

4. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data: 4.1 Account and Profile Data Name, email address, phone number, username, profile photo (if provided), age eligibility confirmation, gender (if voluntarily provided), city/country, language, preferences, interests, and other profile information you choose to submit. 4.2 Matching and Interaction Data Preferences, compatibility inputs, questionnaire responses, interaction history, messaging metadata (timestamps only, not message content unless reported), connections, blocks, reports, and feedback. 4.3 Content You Provide Text, images, and other information you upload, share, or generate through the Platform. 4.4 Technical and Device Data IP address, device type, operating system, browser or app version, identifiers, language settings, logs, and diagnostic data. 4.5 Usage and Analytics Data Pages viewed, features used, session duration, interaction patterns, and general engagement analytics. 4.6 Payment and Subscription Data (If Applicable) Subscription status, plan type, billing history, and payment confirmation. IntellMatch does not store full payment card details. Payments are processed by trusted third-party payment providers.

5. Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

5.1 Sensitive Personal Data IntellMatch does not require sensitive personal data such as government identification numbers, biometric data, health information, precise location, or financial account details. Users must not upload such data unless explicitly requested for a lawful purpose. Any submission of sensitive data is at the User's discretion and risk, and IntellMatch may remove such data to protect compliance and safety. 5.2 Data of Minors The Platform is not intended for individuals below the minimum age displayed at registration. We do not knowingly collect personal data of minors.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data only where a lawful basis exists, including: 6.1 Performance of a contract (providing the Service) 6.2 User consent (where required and withdrawable at any time) 6.3 Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement), balanced against user rights 6.4 Compliance with legal obligations 6.5 Protection of vital interests (rare and exceptional circumstances)

We use personal data to: 7.1 Provide and operate the Platform 7.2 Enable profile creation, matching, recommendations, and messaging 7.3 Improve features, performance, and user experience 7.4 Ensure safety, security, and integrity of the Platform 7.5 Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations 7.6 Communicate service updates, security alerts, and support responses 7.7 Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

IntellMatch does not make decisions producing legal or similarly significant effects solely through automated processing. Users remain solely responsible for their decisions and interactions.

17. Cookie Policy

Introduction This Cookie Policy explains how IntellMatch ("Platform", "we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you access or use our website and applications. This Cookie Policy should be read together with the Privacy Policy. What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, mobile phone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies help platforms recognize devices, remember preferences, and improve functionality and performance. Types of Cookies We Use: Strictly Necessary Cookies These cookies are essential for the operation of the Platform and cannot be disabled. They enable core functions such as: • Account login and authentication • Session management • Security and fraud prevention Without these cookies, the Platform may not function properly. Preference Cookies These cookies allow the Platform to remember your preferences and settings, such as: • Language selection • Interface preferences They enhance usability but are not strictly required for basic functionality. Analytics and Performance Cookies These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Platform by collecting aggregated and anonymized information, such as: • Pages or features used • Usage patterns and performance metrics This information helps us improve the Platform's performance and user experience. Third-Party Cookies Some cookies may be set by trusted third-party service providers (such as analytics or infrastructure providers) acting on our behalf. These providers are contractually bound to use cookies only for authorized purposes and in accordance with applicable data protection laws. We do not use cookies to sell personal data. Legal Basis for Cookie Use Strictly necessary cookies are used based on legitimate interest and contractual necessity. Non-essential cookies (such as analytics or preference cookies) are used where permitted by law and may be subject to your consent, depending on your location and applicable regulations (including GDPR). Managing Cookies You can control or delete cookies through your browser or device settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the availability or functionality of parts of the Platform. Updates to This Cookie Policy We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. Updates will be published on the Platform with an updated effective date.
